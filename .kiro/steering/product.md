# Product Overview

## AI Social Media Replier

AI-powered social media management SaaS platform that generates engaging responses using multiple AI providers (OpenAI GPT, Google Gemini, Mistral AI, Anthropic Claude).

### Core Features
- **Multi-AI Provider Support**: Intelligent fallbacks across OpenAI, Google AI, Mistral, and Anthropic
- **Browser Extensions**: Universal support for Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, Opera
- **Real-time Integration**: Works directly on social platforms with context-aware reply generation
- **Enterprise Features**: Team collaboration, analytics, performance tracking, 2FA security
- **Payment Integration**: Stripe and PayPal support with subscription management
- **Content Creation**: Hashtags, captions, bio optimization with consistent brand voice

### Target Users
- Social media managers
- Content creators
- Marketing teams
- Businesses managing multiple social accounts
- Enterprise teams requiring collaboration features

### Business Model
- SaaS subscription platform
- Usage-based billing with multiple tiers
- Browser extension distribution
- Enterprise licensing
